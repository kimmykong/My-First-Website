Step Two: Finishing the first layer

Now that your cube looks like this , it's time to finish the first layer

The goal is to get the cube looking like

  • The cross should be at the top of the cube
  • Next, you need to find a corner piece, and place it directly under where is should be.
  • Orient the cube so that it is in the bottom right corner
  • If the color of the cross is facing to the left, do R' D' R
  • If the color of the corss is facing to the front of you, do F D F'
  • Continue this until the top layer is solved.
  • Now you can move on to Layer Two

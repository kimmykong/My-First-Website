Step Three: Solving the second layer

Now that your cube looks like this , it's time to solve the second layer

The goal is to get the cube looking like

  • Put the solved side on the bottom.
  • Next make it so that there's an upside down T on one side, with the opposide color needing to go to either the right or left side.
  • If the cube needs to go to the left, as seen in this picture do U' L' U L U F U' F'
  • If it needs to go to the right, as seen in this picture , do U R U'R U'F'U F
  • Keep doing this until your second layer is solved.
  • Now you can move on to Top Cross

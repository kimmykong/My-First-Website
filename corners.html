Step Five: Correct the corners

The goal of thsi step is to get all of the corners in the right place, but not oriented correctly.

Take a look at your cube's corners. You're looking for corners in the right place, with the three color matching the top and two sides' colors.You will either have:

  • Situation one: All corners in the correct place
  • Situation two: One corner in the correct place
  • Situation three: No corners in the correct place

      • IF you have situation one, you can move on to Last Step
      If you have situation three, do U R U' L' U R' U' L and will now be in situation two
      Situation two: Your correct corner shoudl be in the top right corner of the cube. Now preform U R U' L' U R' U' L
      You will now have either all of your corners correct, or still just the same one. If you have the same one correct, repeat the previous step

      Now that all of your corners are in the right place, it's time to solve your cube! You may now move on to Last Step

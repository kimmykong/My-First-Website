Step six: Fix the corners

Now that your corners are in the right place, you must orient them correctly.

***It is very important that you hold the middle of the cube through this step***

In the top right corner, there should be an unoriented corner. Preform R' D' R D TWICE! If it's not oriented, do R' D' R D again

Move the top layer (U) until another unsolved corner is in the top right corner. Do the same thing as before.

When all of your corners are oriented correctly, your cube is a few twists to do. But those are easy and you can figure that out yourself

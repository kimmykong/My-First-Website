Step One: The cross

The first part of solving your cube is to make a cross on one side. THe goal is to get the cube looking like this with each part of the cross matching up with its center

  • First, start off with getting one side of the cross correct, lined up with its correct center
  • If you come into a situation where the cubelet is in the correct place, but not oriented correctly, preform the algorithm: F' U F'
  • When you are finished with this step, you can move on to Step Two

Step Four: The top cross

Now that your cube looks like , it's time to make a cross on the top

  • The bottom of the cube should be the first layer that you solved
  • The top layer may have one of three configurations. The top will look like:
    ∗ ∗ ∗
    ∗ ♥ ∗
    ∗ ∗ ∗


    Picture 1
    ∗ ♥ ∗
    ♥ ♥ ∗
    ∗ ∗ ∗
    Picture 2

    ∗ ∗ ∗
    ♥ ♥ ♥
    ∗ ∗ ∗
    Picture 3 OR

    ∗ ♥ ∗
    ♥ ♥ ♥
    ∗ ♥ ∗
    Picture 4

    The goal is to get the top layer like the last picture. So if you have the top looking like picture 4, you can move on to the next section below.
    If it looks like picture two , move on to step three
    If your top looks like picture three, move on to step 5

    1. If your top layer looks like the first picture, do F R U R' U F'
    2. Now your cube will look like picture 2.
    3. Orient your cube so that the small L is in the top left corner
    4. Preform F R U R' U F'
    5. Next you will have a line in the middle. Orient your cube so that the line is facing horizontally to the front.
    6. Preform F R U R' U F'
    7. Now the top of your cube should look like the last picture.
    8. Now you can move on to part 2

    Your goal of this part is to get all of the sides of the cross lined up with each correct side, kind of like step one.

    1. First move the top layer around a bit and you'll that you have one of three situations:
      1. The top sides of the cross all line up with the correct side
      2. Two sides match up, and are across from one another OR
      3. Two sides match up, next to each other

    Your goal is to end up like situation 1, so if you have that you can move on to Corners
    Situation 2: do R U R' U R 2U R' U
    Now you will be in situation three
    Situation 3: Hold the cube so that one of the correct sides is to the back, and the other to the right and preform R U R' U R 2U R' U

    Only 2 steps left! Move on to Corners
